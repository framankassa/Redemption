Grade 10B Wins Annual Football Tournament!

May 15, 2023

In an electrifying final match that went to penalties, Grade 11D emerged victorious against defending champions Grade 11A. Team captain Yosef Shibeshi scored the winning penalty, securing the trophy for his class. The tournament saw record attendance this year with over 350 students cheering their teams!
