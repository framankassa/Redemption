About Us

Since 2009, the School of Redemption has been a beacon of excellence in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. With over 3000 students and a dedicated team of 400 staff members, we have created a nurturing environment where students thrive academically, socially, and morally. Our motto, "If excellence is possible, good is not enough," drives everything we do.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide a transformative educational experience that empowers students to achieve their highest potential through rigorous academics, character development, and service to humanity, all within a nurturing and inclusive environment.

Our Vision

To be recognized as Ethiopia's premier educational institution that develops visionary leaders and critical thinkers who will drive positive change in their communities and the world at large.

Our Core Values

  • Excellence: We pursue the highest standards in all aspects of education and personal development.
  • Integrity: We uphold honesty, ethics, and moral principles in all our actions.
  • Respect: We value diversity and treat all individuals with dignity and compassion.
  • Responsibility: We foster accountability for our actions and their impact on others.
  • Innovation: We encourage creative thinking and continuous improvement.
  • Community: We build strong relationships and serve our local and global communities.

Our Educational Beliefs

Holistic Development

We believe education should nurture the intellectual, physical, emotional, and social development of every student.

Lifelong Learning

We believe in cultivating curiosity and the love of learning that extends far beyond the classroom.

Student-Centered Approach

We believe each student is unique and deserves personalized attention to unlock their full potential.

Global Citizenship

We believe in preparing students to be responsible citizens of both their local communities and the world.

Our Programs

Kindergarten

Our kindergarten program focuses on play-based learning, helping young minds develop social, emotional, and cognitive skills in a fun and engaging environment.

Elementary School

From Grades 1 to 6, we emphasize foundational skills in literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking, preparing students for the challenges ahead.

High School

Our high school program offers a rigorous academic curriculum, extracurricular activities, and career guidance to help students excel in their chosen paths.
